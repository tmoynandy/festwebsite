About The Panache

The Panache 2017 intends to bring to you a blend of the realistic elements of English language with the magical elements of the fantasy world! We march forward with the aim of providing a platform to our students to articulate to the world that nothing is impossible; with confidence and perseverance, we can create nothing short of magic!

Presenting to you

Events of Panache'17.

The Panache 2017 brings to you 10 unique events for all of you to explore your talents. We give you the platform, you give us your talents! This is the chance to let the entire college know you and to clap for you! So, brace yourselves, as we present to you the events of The Panache, 2017!

Debating Minds

One of the most interesting events, Debate, gives you the voice to ignite minds with your words. If out-talking and out-thinking people is what you're good at, this is the choice you need to make! We give you the moot, you give us your voice! "Chaos is a ladder", let's climb the ladder!

Graffiti

With this event, we aim at bringing out the artistic creativity in our students, for we believe that Art is not what you see, Art is what you make others see! A topic is usually provided and students have to create their art pieces adhering to the stipulated topic.

Just A Minute (JAM)

JAM, being an event which tests not only your vocabulary, or your expertise with public speaking, but also your presence of mind; this event remains a power-packed package! We give you the topic, without pre preparation time, you have to speak on the topic for a minute! If you've the charm and the guts to please the audience with your words within a jiffy, this is the option you're looking out for!

Language Quiz

If you're a cinephile, or a bibliophile, we trust you to rock this event! An event aimed at testing your knowledge of all things English, be it books, or movies or the English language itself, this event challenges you to bring out the best in you! This is basically a general quiz event where you're required to answer questions, mostly based on English language and it's creations!

<

Creative Writing

We at BPPIMT, believe in the adage The Pen is mightier than the Sword! So bring to you an event where you can articulate your convictions with ease! Use the pen to create magic with words and who knows, you might have something special waiting for you!

Skit

This event challenges you with the task of bringing out your imagination and acting skills in a compact time limit of just 4 minutes! So bring out the dramatic side in you and get ready to set the stage on fire with humour, satire, and energy!

Jingle Mingle

Are you a tv buff? Do you enjoy playing with words? If this, then this is the event for you! We provide you a product, you provide us a witty tagline for it! The wittier, the prettier! Pull your socks up, for it's SHOW time!

<

Word Cracker

Are you a spelling veteran? Do wrong spellings or sms language annoy you? This is the event for you, if the answer is yes! Basically a spell bee event, with various rounds leading to the final, this event seeks to test your expertise with words.

Wordakshari

Panache brings to you an event never before heard of, spoof of Antakshari but with Words! This event tests your presence of mind and vocabulary with a series of rounds with separate specifications like, mentioning a list of words starting with the end letter of the previously mentioned word. Being one of the most amusing events of Panache, this remains one of our favourites every year! Chat conversation end Type a message...

Treasure Hunt

Have you always idolised Captain Jack Sparrow? Do you think you've got the brains to crack the clues with your wit and wisdom? Then get set go.. For this is the quest to the treasure hidden somewhere on the campus itself with every clue indicating some faculty members and the staff in the college itself!

Word Cracker

Event type : Individual(prelims) Team(finals)

General Rules : Prelims: Students will have to spell 20 words in 20 minutes when dictated and crack(solve) two words from the given clues in 10 minutes. Total time: 30 minutes. * Top 12 students will be selected from the prelim round. * These 12 students will be divided into 6 teams of 2 each by lottery. Final: There would be total 7 rounds, 5 of which would be based on spelling words and 2 would be based on "Dumb Charades" to crack(solve) for given words.

Event Coordinator : +91

×

Wordakshari

Event type : Team

General Rules :This is a group event and each group will consist of 7-8 students. A specific "subject" or "topic' will be assigned. The starting team have to utter a word related to that specific "topic" and the next team continues with the last letter of that word. Maximum time allowed for a team to answer will be 10 sec after which it passes to the next team. Team with maximum number of correct attempts goes to the next round. 6 teams will be selected to the finals from prelims.

Event Coordinator : +91

Treasure Hunt

Event type : Team

Maximum members : 10

General Rules :Treasure Hunt: A Hunt for letters to form words. Rules: 1.Find the letters to form the words. 2.Each letter will carry a clue. 3.Each clue will crack the answer. 4.Each answer will have a letter. 5.Answer will lead to the BPPIMT staff. 6.Staff checks the letter and answer. If it is correct 7. Staff handovers the next clue with time & initial marked. 8. The group that forms the final words correctly in minimum time.
